Some time has passed after the flash. You feel yourself positioned, lying on a floor.

You try to get up, but somehow — while leveraging yourself up — your hand seems to not be in contact with the floor. Are you near an edge, perhaps? Dangerously lying beside a ledge?

You look around and see that everything around you is pitch black.

You try to sit up instead, but while trying to sit up, your legs seem to phase through the floor as well.

Must you be floating? You seem to not be colliding with anything — anything in this pitch black room. Or maybe this isn't a pitch black room, is this a void with no gravity? But as you awoke, you felt like you were lying on a floor.
