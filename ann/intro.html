Welcome to Mida!

Mida is a browser-based riddle game, heavily inspired by E.B.O.N.Y.. Mida will only have short minimal instructions, assuming you know about E.B.O.N.Y.s rules. A main way to answer in these browser-based riddle games is to change the html link at the bar (intro.html). All you have to change is 'intro' unless an instruction states otherwise.
