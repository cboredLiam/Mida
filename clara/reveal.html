You close your eyes to calm your senses. While your eyes are closed, you feel like you're floating in space, drifting away.

After a while, you feel as if you've come in contact with something, something cold. You open your eyes and you see a ceiling. You find yourself laying down on a cold concrete floor. Looking around, it looks like you're in a hallway.

There are two ends to the hallway, one leads to a door and one leads to a wall, so maybe it's more of a thin room than a hallway. A big message rests on the left wall when facing the door. The door is locked and you predict that it needs an answer.
